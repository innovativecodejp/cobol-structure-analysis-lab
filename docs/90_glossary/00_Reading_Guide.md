# Reading Guide

This glossary defines the terminology used in the COBOL Structure Analysis Lab.
The terms are organized by theoretical layer and theme.

## How to Read

Each term is defined with the following structure:

- **Term**: The name of the concept.
- **Layer**: The abstraction layer where the term is primarily used (Syntax, Structure, Guarantee, Geometry, Decision).
- **Definition**: A concise explanation of the concept.
- **Formal Description**: Mathematical definition or formal notation (if applicable).
- **Related Concepts**: Links to other relevant terms.
- **Example**: A concrete example or diagram.

## Classification Layers

1.  **Syntax Layer**: Concepts related to code parsing and AST.
2.  **Structure Layer**: Concepts related to control flow, data flow, and dependencies.
3.  **Guarantee Layer**: Concepts related to functional equivalence and assurance.
4.  **Geometry Layer**: Concepts related to the mathematical space of migration.
5.  **Decision Layer**: Concepts related to planning, risk, and feasibility.
